Our story

It started with a Model T full of cantaloupe.

In 1937, 25-year-old Harold Boettcher filled his Model T with cantaloupe, sold it along the road to finance the trip, and moved his family to Beloit, Kansas. That willingness to work hard and make the most of every opportunity still runs the place.

More than 80 years later, Boettcher Supply remains a family- and employee-owned business serving contractors, manufacturers and businesses across the region — delivering the quality and service you expect from our experience.
